About Me

@yoshikischmitz

Hi, I'm Yoshiki. I want us to reclaim the liberatory potential of computers. To that end, I am creating research prototypes and concepts of dynamic digital environments for authoring, exploring, communicating, and programming(and hopefully, break down the distinctions between all of these).

Frontiers

These are the questions I'm most excited about, you can click on them to see the beginnings of an answer:

  • What would an operating system that really respected individual autonomy look like?
  • What if there weren't any "apps"- just a single, highly flexible kind of digital document?
  • What if we could transform data into "apps" by directly manipulating the data itself?
  • What if "window management" and "file management" were unified?
  • What if your personal web data was continuous with your desktop?
