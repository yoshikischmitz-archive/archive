Thread-based chat app

This was a concept I was developing for a thread-based chat app.

Benefits:

  • Better Communication:
    • Deeper
    • Focused
    • Organized
    • Uninterrupted
  • Zero friction knowledge base
    • posts are meant to be written in long-form, so you can write them the same way you would a blog post or wiki entry
    • Q&As record which answer was the best and show the entire process of problem resolution in the question and comments
